### Hashes
[[Hash-Funktion]]
$h(x) = x\ mod\ 11$
$r(x) = (x\ mod\ 4) + 1$

|      | $h(x)$ | $r(x)$ |
| ---- | ------ | ------ |
| 1    | 1      | 2      |
| 17   | 6      | 2      |
| 1024 | 1      | 1      |
| 55   | 0      | 4      |
| 59   | 4      | 4      |
| 69   | 3      | 2      |
| 122  | 1      | 3      |


> [!warning] Re-hashing re-computes _another_ Hash for the _original_ Values!!
