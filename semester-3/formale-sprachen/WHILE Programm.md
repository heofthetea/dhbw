## Syntax
- Variablen $x_{0}, x_{1}, x_{2}, ...$
- Konstanten $0, 1, 2, ...$
- Zuweisungsoperator: $:=$
- Kompositionsoperator: $;$
- Arithmetische Operationen $+, \dot{-}$ 
- Keywords `while, do, end` $\leadsto$ while-loop
