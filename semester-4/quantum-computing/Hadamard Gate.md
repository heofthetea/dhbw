> Acts on a singular [[Qubit]]
> creates a [[Superposition]]

$$q \mapsto \begin{cases}\frac{|0\rangle+|1\rangle}{\sqrt{2}}\ if\ q = |0\rangle \\ \frac{|0\rangle-|1\rangle}{\sqrt{2}}\ if\ q = |1\rangle \end{cases}$$

- will have equal [[Wahrscheinlichkeit|probabilities]] of being $|1\rangle$ and $|0\rangle$ 

> [!hint] Combination of two rotations: $180\degree$ around $Z$-axis, followed by $90\degree$ around the $Y$-axis


> [!hint] A double hadamard gate once again neutralizes, just as a [[X-Gate]] would.


## Matrix
$$H = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{bmatrix}1&1 \\ 1& -1\end{bmatrix}$$
