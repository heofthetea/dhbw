[[Qubit]], [[Quantum Object]] 

$$|A\rangle = a |0\rangle + b |1\rangle$$

- isolate (= extract) states makes the [[Quantum Object]] choose one state: 
	- Logical 0 $\equiv |0\rangle = \begin{bmatrix}1 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{C}^{2}$ 
	- Logical 1 $\equiv |1\rangle = \begin{bmatrix}0 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \in \mathbb{C}^{2}$ 
- $a$ and $b$ are [[Wahrscheinlichkeit|Probabilities]]: $a, b \in \mathbb{C},\ \ \ |a|^{2} + |b|^{2} = 1$ ([[Einheitskreis]])
	- $P(|0\rangle) \rightarrow |a|^{2}$
	- $P(|1\rangle) \rightarrow |b|^{2}$
