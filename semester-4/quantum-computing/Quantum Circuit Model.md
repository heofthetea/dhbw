[[Boolsche Funktion|Boolean function]] with [[Qubit|Qubits]]

> [!hint] $n$ Qubits go in, $n$ go out - no new qubits are created, none are destroyed.

- Result is measured - once measured, the [[Superposition]] state is gone.
- There are no [[WHILE while loop|loops]]!

> [!hint] All steps up to the measurement are reversible.

> [!info] Quantum Computing is [[Turing Completeness|Turing Complete]].
