A system with $n$ [[Qubit]] can have $2^{n}$ different possible states.

- uses [[Tensor Product]] to represent the states -> $2^{n+1}$-dimensional [[Vektor]]
## Example: n = 2
$$a \cdot |00\rangle + b \cdot |01\rangle + c \cdot |10\rangle + d \cdot |11\rangle$$
$$|a|^{2} + |b|^{2} + |c|^{2}+ |d|^{2} = 1$$
