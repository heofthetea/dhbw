> MIsst die Strom-Effizienz eines [[Data Center]]


$$\text{PUE} = \frac{\text{total data center power}}{\text{IT equipment power}}$$

- total data center power: Alles, was Das Data Center braucht
	- inklusive Strom, etc.
- IT equipment power: Was nur das IT Equipment verbraucht
