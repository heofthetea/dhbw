> structure-preserving [[Abbildung|Mapping]] from one [[Category]] $C$ into a [[Category]] $D$

$$F : C \longrightarrow D = \begin{cases} \qquad\quad\ \ \,a\mapsto F\,a \\ f: a \rightarrow b \ \mapsto\ F\,{f}: F\,{a} \rightarrow F\,{b} \end{cases}$$
- ($a, b$ are [[Object|objects]], $a \rightarrow b$ is a [[Morphism]] from $a$ to $b$)

> [!hint] $F\,{a}$ is called the "image of $a$" 


![[Pasted image 20251026133442.png]]

## Structure-preserving
1. $F$ preserves [[Identity Morphism|Identity]]:

$$F\,{id_{a}} = id\,{F_{a}}$$
2. $F$ preserves [[Composition]]:

$$F\,({g \circ f}) \mapsto F\,{g} \circ F\,{f}$$
