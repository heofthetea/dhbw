## Definition
- A [[Category]] consists of a [[Menge|Set]] of [[Object|objects]] and a [[Menge|Set]] of [[Morphism|Morphisms]].
- Within a category, two things must always be present:
	1. An [[Identity Morphism]] for every [[Object]]
	2. [[Composition]] must be enforced
