> A [[Coproduct]] of two [[Object|Objects]] $a, b$ is the object $c$ equipped with two [[Morphism|Injections]] $i, j$ such that 

- [[Duality]] of the [[Product]] 
- something like an [[XOR]] -> $c$ is either $a$ or $b$ (terminology wandered out of CT there lol)
