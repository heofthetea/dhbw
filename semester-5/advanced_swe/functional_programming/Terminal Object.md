> The [[Object]] $i \in C$ with **exactly** one [[Morphism]] any other [[Object]] $o \in C$ to $i$

> [!hint] This is just the [[Initial Object]] in the [[Opposite Category]].

[[Absorbierendes Element]]
## Examples
- The [[Leere Menge|Empty Set]] in the [[Category]] of [[Menge|Sets]] (there's only one function $f: X \longrightarrow \varnothing$ for all $X$)
