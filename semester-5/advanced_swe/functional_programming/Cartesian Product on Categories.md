$$C \times D$$
- the [[Class]] of [[Object|objects]] $ob(E)$ is $ob(C) \times ob(D)$ ([[Kartesisches Produkt]])
- A [[Morphism]] in $E$ represents a pair of morphisms $(f, f')$


## Identity
- The [[Identity Morphism]] is

$$id = (id, id)$$

## Composition
-  A pair of morphism composes intuitively:
$$(f, g) \circ (f', g')  = (f \circ g, f' \circ g')$$
