[[Functor]]

- Every [[Object]] is a [[Functor]]

## Identity
- The [[Functor]] corresponding to [[Identity Morphism|Identity]] is an [[Endofunctor]].

## Composition
- [[Functor|Functors]] compose:

$$F: A \longrightarrow B,\ G: B \longrightarrow C$$

$$G \circ F: A \longrightarrow C$$

(where $A, B, C$ are [[Category|Categories]])
