> A [[Abbildung|map]] of an [[Object]] within a [[Category]] to another [[Object]]

$$m: A \longrightarrow B$$
where $A, B$ are [[Object|objects]] and $A$ is called the source, while $B$ is the target.

> [!hint] Think of a [[Morphism]] as a sort of [[Abbildung|function]] [[Function Signature|Signature]].

## Example
- A [[Function]] is a [[Morphism]] in the [[Category]] of [[Menge|Sets]]
