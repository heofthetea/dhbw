> Basically an old version of [[SSH]]

- [[TCP]] [[IANA Well-known Ports|well-known port]] 23
- Intended to give access to a remote terminal (like [[SSH]])

> [!warning] Transmits data (by default) over plain text (no encryption)


> [!hint] According to the [[Netcat]] man page, you can use telnet to [[TCP]]-connect to arbitrary ports.
